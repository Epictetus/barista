Barista

Simple CoffeeScript for Rails and Rack based applications

Barista is a library that makes it as simple as humanely possible to support CoffeeScript in both Rails and Rack applications. Built from the ground up to be as simple as possible, Barista will come naturally if you've ever used Compass for Sass stylesheets.

Getting Started

Depending on what you're working with, adding Barista to your application is generally a pretty easy affair.
